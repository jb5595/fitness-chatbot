About PunchLine

PunchLine is a chatbot designed for specialty gyms (e.g., boxing, CrossFit) to manage client communications via SMS and voice calls. We help gym owners by answering client FAQs (e.g., pricing, trial schedules) and facilitating trial bookings.

SMS Opt-In Process

Users can opt-in to receive SMS messages from PunchLine in the following ways:

1. Opt-In via Voice Call

Users call our toll-free number (+14155238886) and are prompted with:

"Thanks for calling! Press 1 if you would like to receive a text response."

By pressing 1, users provide express consent to receive SMS messages. They receive a text like:

"Thanks for calling [Gym Name]! How can we assist you today? Reply here."

2. Opt-In via SMS with Keyword

Users can text the keyword JOIN to our toll-free number (+14155238886) to opt-in.

Where to Find the Keyword: The keyword "JOIN" is promoted on gym websites, social media pages (e.g., Instagram, Facebook), and physical signage at the gym (e.g., posters or flyers).

Upon texting "JOIN," users receive:

"Welcome to [Gym Name]! You’re now subscribed to SMS updates. Reply with questions (e.g., 'What’s your pricing?') or 'STOP' to opt-out."

3. Opt-In via Direct SMS

Users can text our toll-free number directly (e.g., "Book a trial"). By initiating the conversation, they provide express consent to receive SMS replies, such as:

"Let's get you scheduled! Book here: https://calendly.com/jacobberman1995"

Opt-Out Process

Users can opt-out at any time by replying "STOP" to any SMS message. This unsubscribes them from future messages.
